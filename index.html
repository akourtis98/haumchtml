HAU MARKETING CLUB




We Host Talks with Influential People

Our signature Business Talks series has been the star of our story since 2019. With the best interest of our driven community in mind, we host bi-monthly events with renowned businesspeople, influencers, company executives and many more! Successful professionals take on the club stage and share their stories, inspire, empower and promote our hard-working and ambitious members.

We Hold Hands-On, Innovative Workshops

Our club members get to experience real-time, hands-on business challenges, with a plethora of creative workshops being organized to professionally benefit our young representatives! With time-sensitive projects at hand that reach from “home-made” marketing campaigns to global-scale business competitions, our members try and train their existing skills, while acquiring new ones!

We Make You the Business Champion

Some of our most exciting features include the bi-monthly Business Champion Series, including our special Business Champion Battles! Aspiring members take on the stage and challenge both themselves and their companions in bold singleton speeches or dynamic group brawls: fierce advocates (or brutal critics) of companies and products debate their way to the top in favor or against brands, claiming the title of our honored Business Champion each semester

We Have a Strong Voice

Our club has firmly established its social media and press presence, with talented members behind creative screens and seams in all sorts of places – proudly earning our voice is everywhere. As a member, you are eligible to become an honorary author in the monthly Titan Chronicles Newsletter, with the opportunity to write for the club and be featured in special issues to-be-read by hundreds each month! Grab the opportunity to demonstrate your skills to peers, academics and professionals through our various network resources!.
