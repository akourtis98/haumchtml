WHO WE ARE



The Hellenic American University Marketing Club is a team of motivated students that seek to build a forum that connects motivated students who share a passion for Business.


Our objective is the the cultivation of an inclusive and safe space for all. This encourages co-operation, and in the context of today's competitive ecosystem of Business, that is key for succes. Therefore, our club is an incubator for entrepreneurial talent and inter-personal growth of our members, all on the basis of having a good time.


To achieve our goal, we incorporate experiential learning through group activities, trips, game nights, meet-ups, talks, projects, participating in competitions, and much more!


If you become part of us, you will enhance skills such as teamwork, communication, marketing, soft skills, leadership, and many more!


Join us today to become part of our vision for a top leading business club!
